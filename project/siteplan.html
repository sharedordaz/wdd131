Psychology Office Website Plan

Site Name

MindSpace Therapy

This name reflects a safe and calm digital space where individuals can learn about therapy options, values, and connect with a professional psychologist.

Optional domain availability: mindspacetherapy.org

Site Purpose

The website will present the therapies available, share the professional values and moral foundation of the psychologist, and include a simple form to help people register for consultations.

Scenarios

  • What types of therapy does this psychologist offer?
  • How can I schedule a session or register for therapy?
  • What are the professional values and approach of this psychologist?

Color Schema

Main Blue: #09b5a5 — Used for headings and key accents.

Secondary Gray: #595959 — Used for text or muted content.

White: #f2e9dc — Used for main background text.

Green: #cfd11a — Used for borders or call-to-action sections.

Accent Orange: #f06543 — Used for links, buttons, and hover effects.

Typography

Headings: Georgia – Creates a sense of professionalism and trust.

Body Text: Segoe UI – Clean and modern, ideal for readability.
